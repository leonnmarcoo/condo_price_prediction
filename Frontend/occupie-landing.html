What We Cooked Inside OccuPie

OccuPie isn't just a tasty name—it's packed with features that make condo predictions smarter, clearer, and a whole lot more fun.

Find Your Slice
on the Map

Type in a condo, and OccuPie serves up its location along with nearby establishments—so you see not just the unit, but the community around it.

Slice the Numbers
with AI

Using MLR for condo pricing and Logistic Regression for occupancy demand, OccuPie turns raw data into clear, reliable predictions.

A Bigger Bite
with LLMs

Beyond numbers, OccuPie uses language models to analyze and explain results—making insights easy to understand, even without a math degree.
